1.2 Activation function

1.2.1 Commonly used activation functions:

  • sigmoid: \sigma(x) = \frac{1}{1+e^{-x}}

  • tanh: tanh(x) = 2 \sigma(2x) - 1

  • ReLU: ReLU(x) = max(0, x)

  • Softmax: z_i \rightarrow \frac{e^{z_i}}{\sum_{j=1}^{k} e^{z_j}}
